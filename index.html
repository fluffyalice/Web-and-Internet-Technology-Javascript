This is an art gallery website, and if you click the bottom named “Alignment”, you can see the gallery lays out a row of five pictures with different frames. If you click the bottom named “Random”, you can see the gallery randomly arranges the pictures and frames. If you click the bottom named “Collection”, you can see the five pictures are positioned with one picture in the middle of the canvas and the other four pictures displayed near each corner of the one in the middle. If you click the bottom of “Frames”, the pictures and their particular arrangement should stay the same but the picture frames should change to different picture frames.
